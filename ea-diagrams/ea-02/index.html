Architecture Notes

This diagram traces every data handoff along the complete Royal Caribbean guest journey. A booking in the RES system triggers a chain of downstream events spanning pre-cruise communications, terminal check-in, IDEMIA MFACE facial recognition, SeaPass account activation, 5+ days of onboard spend capture via Oracle MICROS, final folio settlement, IDEMIA-assisted debarkation, and Crown & Anchor loyalty accrual — all feeding back into Salesforce CRM and SAP for post-voyage accounting.

  • RES is the master booking record — all downstream systems derive guest identity from it
  • eDocs processing triggers 72 hours before departure; Royal App push notification sent
  • IDEMIA MFACE processes facial templates uploaded at check-in; debarkation <8 seconds per guest
  • SeaPass RFID links physical card to SPMS folio; every onboard charge posts in <2 seconds
  • Crown & Anchor loyalty points calculated from voyage spend and posted within 48h of disembarkation
